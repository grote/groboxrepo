WARNING: This is an unofficial alpha version that is not secure and might break with every update.

When an update kills your database, say that you forgot your password to create a fresh database.

Briar is a messaging app designed for activists, journalists,
and anyone else who needs a safe, easy and robust way to communicate.
Unlike traditional messaging tools such as email, Twitter or Telegram,
Briar doesn't rely on a central server - messages are synchronized directly between the users' devices.
If the Internet's down, Briar can sync via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, keeping the information flowing in a crisis.
If the Internet's up, Briar can sync via the Tor network, protecting users
and their relationships from surveillance.
